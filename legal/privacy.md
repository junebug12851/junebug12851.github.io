<p><em>Last updated: July 23, 2026</em></p>

<p><strong>The short version.</strong> This is the privacy policy for the
<strong>fairyfox.io</strong> hub — the project home and documentation library. It has no
accounts and collects no personal information about you. Your reading preferences and your
Fairy&nbsp;Fox coins are saved only in your own browser. The site sets no cookies and runs
no analytics or tracking. Its fonts and icons are self-hosted, so it makes no third-party
request to render a page. Each linked project has its own policy.</p>

<h2>What this covers</h2>
<p>This policy explains how the <strong>fairyfox.io</strong> site
(&ldquo;the Site&rdquo;, &ldquo;we&rdquo;, &ldquo;us&rdquo;) handles information. The Site is
a free, static website — the hub and documentation library for 1fairyfox&rsquo;s software
work — served at <a href="https://fairyfox.io/">fairyfox.io</a>. It runs entirely in your
browser. Individual projects reached from the Site (games, apps, and other tools) have their
own policies; this one covers the hub itself.</p>

<h2>What we do not collect</h2>
<ul>
<li>There are <strong>no user accounts</strong> and no sign-up. We never ask for your name,
email, phone number, or address.</li>
<li>We run <strong>no analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or behavioral
profiling</strong>, and we build no user database.</li>
<li>We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.</li>
<li>We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with anyone, because we hold none.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Information stored on your own device</h2>
<p>To make the Site nicer to use, it saves a little information in your browser — not on any
server:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Reading preferences</strong> — the &ldquo;Aa&rdquo; reading menu&rsquo;s theme,
accent colour, text size, line spacing, and width.</li>
<li><strong>Fairy&nbsp;Fox coins</strong> — a small reading-engagement counter: your coin
balance and a record of which pages you have opened <em>today</em> (so a page is only counted
once a day). This resets its daily record each day; the balance carries over.</li>
</ul>
<p>Both are kept in your browser&rsquo;s <strong>local storage</strong> and stay on your
device; they are never transmitted to us. They are saved under shared keys so a single choice
and one coin balance carry across the same-origin <a href="https://fairyfox.io/">fairyfox.io</a>
site in the same browser — still local to your device only. <strong>We provide an easy way to
clear or reset this data:</strong> the reading menu has a <strong>Reset</strong>, the coins panel
has a <strong>Clear my data</strong> link, and you can also clear it any time from your
browser&rsquo;s site-data settings for the Site — any of which simply resets your saved preferences
and coins.</p>

<h2>Hosting and server logs</h2>
<p>The Site is served as static files by GitHub Pages. Like virtually all web hosting, the
provider automatically processes basic technical information required to deliver the site and
keep it secure — most notably your IP address and standard request metadata — in its server
logs. This is handled by
<a href="https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement">GitHub</a>
as our hosting provider under its own privacy policy. We add no analytics, profiles, or user
database on top of it.</p>

<h2>Fonts, icons, and third-party requests</h2>
<p>The Site's <strong>typefaces and icon set are self-hosted</strong> — both the web fonts (Fraunces,
Inter, JetBrains Mono) and the Font&nbsp;Awesome icon font are served from fairyfox.io's own origin.
Loading a page makes <strong>no request to Google Fonts, cdnjs, or any other third-party provider</strong>
for presentation, so no font/icon CDN sees your IP address. The Site embeds no ad networks, analytics
scripts, or social widgets. (The one unavoidable third party is our host, GitHub Pages, which receives
standard request logs to serve the Site at all — see below.)</p>

<h2>Cookies</h2>
<p>The Site sets <strong>no cookies</strong>. It uses your browser&rsquo;s local storage for the
preferences and coins described above, which is not a tracking cookie and is not transmitted to
us. See our <a href="{{ '/legal/cookies/' | relative_url }}">Cookies Policy</a> for details.</p>

<h2>Children&rsquo;s privacy</h2>
<p>The Site is a general-audience documentation and project site. Because we collect no personal
information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect it from children. If you believe a child has
sent us personal information — for example, by emailing us — please contact us and we will delete
it.</p>

<h2>Your privacy rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and others)</h2>
<p>Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your
personal information, to object to or restrict its processing, and to not be discriminated
against for exercising those rights. Because we hold essentially no personal information about
you, there is usually nothing on our side to access or delete — the data that exists (your
reading preferences and coins) is on your own device and fully under your control.</p>
<p><strong>For California residents (CCPA/CPRA):</strong> we do not collect, sell, or
&ldquo;share&rdquo; (for cross-context behavioral advertising) your personal information, and we
use no sensitive personal information.</p>
<p><strong>For users in the EEA/UK (GDPR):</strong> where any limited processing occurs (such as
the hosting logs described above), it is based on our
legitimate interest in operating and securing the Site. You have the right to lodge a complaint
with your local data protection authority.</p>

<h2>Security</h2>
<p>We take reasonable steps to keep the Site secure, but no method of storage or transmission
over the internet is completely secure. The Site runs client-side and we operate no server that
holds your data.</p>

<h2>International users</h2>
<p>The Site can be used worldwide. When you load it, your request and the limited technical data
described above may be processed in the United States or other countries where our hosting and
the third-party providers operate.</p>

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
<p>We may update this Privacy Policy as the Site changes. When we do, we will revise the
&ldquo;Last updated&rdquo; date above. Significant changes will be reflected here on this page.</p>

<h2>Contact us</h2>
<p>If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at
<a href="mailto:fairy@fairyfox.io">fairy@fairyfox.io</a>.</p>
