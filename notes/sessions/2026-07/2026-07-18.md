to avoid a flash. It's a framework-agnostic contract every sibling can satisfy. On the hub no
page sets it (Stories/Games are redirect stubs to the sibling sites), so here the two controls
stay locked with the note ‚Äî exactly "the individual updates are where they un-disable."
