## Force-push root-cause fix — full-history mirrors, no phantom `reset --hard` (0.9.6)

Live session (Fairy Fox present). Began as the automated **inbound report-review pass**
(read 3 new sibling reports: pokered `…check-for-updates.md`; RAP `…adopting-updates-2/-3.md`),
which produced a findings briefing. Fairy Fox then pushed back — hard — on the standing
"force-push is routine" framing: *there should never be a force-push, fix the problem
instead of working around it.* They are right, and the diagnosis bore it out.

**Diagnosis (PowerShell + editor tools, no bash).** The recurring "hub `dev` was
force-pushed" reports were a **misdiagnosis**. Evidence:

- The hub's `dev` is provably linear — old markers (`42b6ea4`, `6777a73`, `7ae3f08`) are
  all still ancestors of HEAD; `--no-ff` merges, no rewrites. `git-workflow.md` already
  forbids force-push. So the "force-pushed routinely" claim in the sync standards
  **contradicted** the hard rule.
- The mirrors were **`--depth 1` shallow** clones. Reproduced on the disposable RAP
  mirror: roll local `dev` back one real commit, `fetch --depth 1`, then `merge --ff-only`
  → `fatal: refusing to merge unrelated histories` (the shallow graft has no merge base).
  That abort is what the procedure misread as a force-push and answered with `reset --hard`.
- After `git fetch --unshallow`, `1868b58` is confirmed a true ancestor of `cb805ef` and
  the **same refresh fast-forwards cleanly**. No force-push anywhere — just a shallow clone.

**The fix (standards, hub-side).** Removed every "force-pushed routinely" / `--depth 1` /
routine-`reset --hard` instruction across the sync standards, runbooks, templates, READMEs,
the `.last-seen.yml` header, and the hub's adopted `notes/reference/cross-project-sync.md`.
Reference mirrors are now **single-branch full-history** clones; refresh is a plain
`fetch` + `merge --ff-only`; an `--ff-only` abort is an **anomaly to diagnose**
(deepen/re-clone a stale shallow mirror), never a destructive routine. A real
non-fast-forward = someone rewrote `dev` → stop and investigate.

**Local mirrors** unshallowed (`git fetch --unshallow`); pokered + RAP both fast-forward
cleanly now. PATCH release `0.9.5 → 0.9.6`.

Not touched: `reports_through` markers (the 3 new reports remain for a proper fold-in
session — this fix came from the live diagnosis, not from digesting them); RAP `cb805ef`
is still un-blogged 2026-06-27 work for the next round-up. The earlier inbound briefing's
"embrace force-push as routine" suggestion is **superseded** by this root-cause fix.
