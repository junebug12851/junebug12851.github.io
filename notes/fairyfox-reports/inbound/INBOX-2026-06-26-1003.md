  constraint checks**, run **before and after**, with a hard fallback — *if full
  verification can't be completed, do not auto-apply; revert to check-report-wait.*

  **verification floor**: (i) reconcile without clobbering, (ii) build + tests,
  (iii) the relevant `## Verify` / compliance checks, (iv) project-constraint check —

### B. Make the `reports_through` marker robust to same-day reports — **MEDIUM, one-off (process/meta)**
